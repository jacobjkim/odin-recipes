Sweet Potato Chili Recipe

This is a great recipe for a hearty and nutricious vegan chili recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Large onion
  • 1 Clove of garlic
  • 3 Sweet Potatoes
  • 1/4 cup Chili Seasoning
  • 1 Can of black beans
  • Fresh herbs of your choice
  • 1 Can diced tomatoes
  • A pinch of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Saute vegetables: This includes onion, garlic, sweet potatoes.
  2. Add other ingredients: This includes diced tomatoes, broth, water, chili powder, cumin powder, salt, pepper, red beans, black beans.
  3. Mix unitl comined
  4. Simmer uncovered until all tghe veggies are cooked
  5. Garnish with fresh herbs and enjoy!
